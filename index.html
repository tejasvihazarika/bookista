Welcome to bookista

Explore the beauty of Reading

Fiction

Fiction encompasses a wide range of subgenres, including historical fiction, contemporary fiction, literary fiction, and more. It focuses on narrative storytelling with imaginative elements.

Mystery & Thriller

This genre includes suspenseful stories with plot twists, investigations, and often, a crime or puzzle to solve. It keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

Science Fiction

Explore futuristic worlds, advanced technology, and speculative scenarios that challenge the imagination. From space exploration to dystopian societies, science fiction offers a wide range of thought-provoking themes and adventures.

Non-Fiction

Non-fiction includes factual books based on real events, people, and knowledge. This genre spans biographies, memoirs, self-help, history, and more.

Romance

Romance novels focus on romantic relationships between characters. This genre often includes themes of love, intimacy, and emotional connection.

Young Adult (YA)

YA books are written for and about teenagers and young adults. They often address themes of identity, coming of age, and personal growth, with various subgenres like dystopian, fantasy, and contemporary.

Fantasy

Magical worlds, mythical creatures, and epic quests that transport readers to realms filled with wonder, magic, and adventure.

Historical Fiction

Stories set in the past that blend fictional narratives with real historical events, offering a glimpse into different eras and cultures.
